iQAS vision

iQAS is a platform that allows to integrate many observation sources (physical, virtual or logical sensors). Based on the actor model, iQAS is autonomic, extensible and configurable, allowing Smart City stakeholders to collaboratively assess and improve (when possible) QoO in real-time.
