About the SENSE project

The iQAS platform, Virtual Sensor Containers (VSCs) and Virtual Application Consumers (VACs) have been developed during the PhD thesis of Antoine Auger at ISAE-SUPAERO (2014-2017).
This research was supported in part by the French Ministry of Defence through financial support of the Direction Générale de l’Armement (DGA).
